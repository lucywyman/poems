A Love Letter to California

I think I fall in love too often
Or...think about falling in love too often
Thinking too fast
or falling too fast
I’m not sure which
I think I love you
I think about lying in your arms as I lay in someone else’s
I think about thinking about you when there’s nothing else to fill the space
You were my ecstasy, six days of high for six weeks of
utter helplessness and
I wish that I was stronger
that I could beat this addiction to a chemical reaction
that to you I was more than just a sexual transaction
When I said I wanted to experiment with drugs this wasn’t what I meant.
I didn’t mean to be intoxicated by your smile
Or get high on stardust as we lay beneath the moon
I never imagined myself pulling my sleeve down, trying to hide the scars you left from my friends
becoming a junkie
but the feeling of you rushing through my veins left me breathless
always craving more, until more was never enough, but...

But I guess it’s true what they say
that the best laid plans of mice and men often go astray
and in a way it’s been a blessing
a reminder that for every high there’s a low
that there are a thousand people who feel this way too,
That I am still human
my heart is still beating
a reminder that I will always have good friends, chai tea and spongebob squarepants
that this life is beautiful
and that you don’t need a hit to enjoy it.
