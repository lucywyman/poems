Day 3: Ode to Tomato Soup

Oh, Tomato Soup You have always been tehre for me Through thick and thin, you were there Sometimes thick, sometimes thin, but always warm (Except for that one time in the dorms, but I don't hold it against you) Tomato Soup, you and your tireless sidekick Grilled Cheese Are the very picture of perfection Envied by all other culinary creations Even Pizza cannot compete
