What love is (working title)

Some days, love is a kiss goodnight
Some days it's sunshine and ice cream and singing "In the Summertime, when the weather is high" with the windows rolled down
Or the text you get before your plane takes off
Some days love is the hand you feel on the small of your back
The smell of his hair on your clothes at the end of the day
The way he says "You look beautiful"
Some days love is saying "He was a jerk anyway"
And being there with gummy bears, tissues, and seasons 2-4 of Bridezillas
Or giving her the chocolate you brought to school because lord knows she needs it more than you do.
Some days, love is sitting next to someone in a movie theater and not holding their hand
Some days it's swallowing the words "I love you", and just being a friend
And holding your breath when they get too close because at least then you can focus on how bad your lungs are burning instead of how much you want to kiss them
Some days it's not speaking
Glancing down when you see the person you love across the room
Letting them extracate themself from your life
Some days love is convincing him to get out of bed in the morning and eat something
Others it's letting him sleep
Some days, love is the email you get after six months of silence that reads "I'm sorry I mistreated you"
Some days love says "I got you an adorable yellow dress for Easter. I hope it fits. I miss you."
My mom always told me that love was finding someone who didn't drive you nuts
While my dad always said there was no such thing as love
But I've found that neither of them were quite right.
Love is not always what we expect it to be.
It doesn't always come when we want it to
But it will always be there.
